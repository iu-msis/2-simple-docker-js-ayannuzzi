When Were Books First Used?

Books were first used around 2000BC by the Sumarians in Mesopatamia. They used to write on clay tablets in cuneiform.

The Papyrus scroll with written words originated in Egypt around 2400BC.

In 600 BC, standardized writing systems evolved.

Definition of Books

According to Merriam-Webster, a book is a set of written, printed, or blank sheets bound together between a front and back cover.
